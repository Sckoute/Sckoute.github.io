MANCHESTER

The event will be held at the Machester Centre, famous for hosting events such as ComicCon. The event will run from the 4th of August to the 8th of August and each day of the event will be held from 10am to 10pm. Day tickets will cost £50 and a ticket for all 4 days will cost £180.

MAIN STAGE

The main stage of the event is where all of the major tournaments will happen along with the major annoucements inbetween. There will be a tournament per day from Thursday to Sunday in the order of Smash Bros Melee, then Overwatch, then DotA 2 and then finally Smash 4. Each tournament will be followed up with a major announcement.

AROUND THE VENUE

Away from the main stage there will be plenty of other things to do such as shopping at stalls, playing new and old games, meeting up with you favourite indie game developers and some exclusive indie game annoucements.
